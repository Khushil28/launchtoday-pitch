Market Timing

Why Now? The Landscape is Tightening

MCA Scrutiny Increasing

Ministry of Corporate Affairs has automated scrutiny. Director disqualification (DIR-3) and shell company strikes are now automated triggers.

GST Penalties & Audits

GST registrations are being cancelled for non-filing. Mismatches in Input Tax Credit (ITC) now lead to immediate working capital freezes.

Funding Winter = Deeper DD

Investors are risk-averse. "Messy cap tables" or missing secretarial records are no longer overlooked—they are deal-breakers.

DPDP Act Implications

The new Digital Personal Data Protection Act imposes penalties up to ₹250 Cr. Startups need robust data governance frameworks now.

Incubators cannot afford sloppy governance anymore.

The era of "move fast and break things" does not apply to compliance.
